## ğŸ›‘ How to Play

1.  **Launch the game** and press `START` from the Main Menu.
2.  **Last Saved level** begin playing.
3.  Use the **on-screen controls** to navigate your car through the maze.
    *   **Arrow Buttons:** Control acceleration, braking, and steering.
4.  **Avoid colliding** with the obstacles that form the maze walls.
5.  **Park your car** in the designated parking spot before the timer runs out.
6.  **Succeed** to earn coins and unlock the next level!
